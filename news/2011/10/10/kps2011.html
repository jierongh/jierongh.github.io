A report of The 1st Korean Protein Society Symposium

  • October 10, 2011
  • Young Kee Kang

The First Korean Protein Society Symposium was held at the Faculty House of Seoul National University in Seoul on September 23, 2011. The total number of about 100 audiences participated at The Symposium. The Symposium had three plenary speakers-Professor Se Won Suh (Seoul National University), Professor Yuji Goto (Osaka University), and Professor Zengyi Chang (Peking University), 12 invited speakers, and poster presentation. The Symposium was composed of three sections, i.e., protein structure, protein function, and protein application. Each section had four invited speakers. About 40 papers were presented by posters, from which three were awarded as the Best Poster Presentation. This symposium was sponsored by Dong-il Shimadzu, Roche, Agilent, and Bruker.
